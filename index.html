Our Story

Built on Trust,
Roofed with Pride

Founded in 1999 by master roofer James Carter, ProRoof Elite started as a two-man crew in a pickup truck with one simple promise: do the job right the first time, every time.

Twenty-five years later, we've grown into Austin's most trusted roofing company — but that same promise is still at the heart of everything we do. We never cut corners, never use inferior materials, and never leave a job site until our client is completely satisfied.
